    Project management business documents:

    1. Project business case: Created and maintained by the Project sponsor. Its the economic feasibility study and validates the benifits of the project. It is used to make decision on project selection and also it is maintained through out the project for project management decisions and actions.

    3. Project management plan:
    4. Benefits management plan: Document used to define creating, maximising and sustaining project benefits. Mainly focuses on Target benefits, Strategic alignment and Timeframes. Includes metrics, assumptions and risks.
